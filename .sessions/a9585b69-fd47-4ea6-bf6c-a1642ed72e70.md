**What rcodesign is, plainly:**

macOS apps include a cryptographic signature inside the `.app` bundle. Apple's tool for creating that signature is called `codesign` but it only runs on macOS because it's part of Xcode.

`rcodesign` is an independent open-source rewrite in Rust that does the exact same job and **runs on Linux**. Written by Gregory Szorc ([github.com/indygreg/apple-platform-rs](https://github.com/indygreg/apple-platform-rs)), single static binary, no dependencies.

rcodesign can produce ad-hoc signatures from Linux, solving the Apple Silicon launch problem with no Apple account.

**Analogy:** it's like a tamper-evident seal on a package. A real identity signature has a FedEx logo on the seal proving who shipped it.
